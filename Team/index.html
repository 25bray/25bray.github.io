What is Epilepsy

  • Epilepsy is a brain disorder
  • It causes you to have seizures
  • Most of the time these seizures are caused by flashing lights and other sudden movements.

    • There are a lot of kinds of seizures such as a

  • Tonic-Clonic Seizure
  • Tonic Seizure
  • Clonic Seizure
  • Absence Seizure

    • What is a EEG

  • Electroencephalography
  • This is how doctors can see the electrical activity in your brain

    • What is the Seizure First Aid

  • STAY with the person until they are awake and alert after the seizure. Time the seizure Remain calm Check for medical ID
  • Keep the person SAFE. Move or guide away from Harm
  • Turn the person onto their SIDE if they are not awake or aware. Keep airway clear Loosen tight clothes around neck Put something small and soft under their head
  • Call 911 if... Seizure lasts longer that 5 minutes Person does not return to their usual state Person is injured, prenant, or sick Repeated seizures First time seizure Difficulty breathing Seizure occurs in water
  • DO NOT Do NOT restrain. Do NOT put any objects in their mouth. Rescue Medications can be given if prescribed by a health care professional

    • What is a Seizure

  • During a seizure, there are bursts of electrical activity happening in your brain.
  • But seizures are different for everyone that actually has Epilepsy
